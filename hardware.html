Hardware Engeneer

What I can do:


- Diagnose and troubleshoot failed rotational media and solid-state storage devices;
- Formatting computers;
- Software installation;
- Diagnosis of computer anomalies;
- Computer repair;
- Modify hardware to enable forensic analysis of the media or to change the functionality of the hardware for other purposes;
- Hardware Cleaning.

Computer Hardware Engineer Skills & Competencies:


Critical thinking:
Comparing various solutions to problems will allow you to identify the best one.

Reading comprehension:
You'll have to do a lot of reading to keep up with changing technology.

Communication skills:
Computer hardware engineers frequently work on teams, so excellent verbal communication and listening skills are essential.

Creativity:
You must be able to come up with new ideas for designing computers, devices, and peripherals.
